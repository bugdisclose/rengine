Support for Slack

Currently the notification service works only with slack. While in the future we might bring support for collaboration tools like Microsoft Teams and others, as of now we do not have any immediate plans to integrate with other collaboration platform.

If you want to learn more on how to get the slack web hook URL, please follow this guide. https://api.slack.com/messaging/webhooks

Please note that, your slack web hook url must be in the format of https://hooks.slack.com/services/T00000000/B00000000/XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX
